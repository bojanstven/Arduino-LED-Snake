Game Controls:

Keyboard

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move snake
  • Hold direction: Speed boost
  • Space Bar: Restart after game over

Gamepad

  • D-Pad or Left Stick: Move snake
  • Push stick harder for speed boost
  • L/ZL buttons: Decrease speed level
  • R/ZR buttons: Increase speed level
  • A/B/X/Y buttons: Restart

Level up by eating 5 food items. There are no walls - you wrap around. Don't hit yourself!
